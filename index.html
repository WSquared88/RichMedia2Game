Rich Media 2 Project

Abstract

My game is a strategic card game where you and an opponent play cards on a board and have them attack each other. The first player to destroy the opponents leader wins.

High Level Concept

My game is a card game where you place the cards onto a board and have them move and attack each other. Players have a deck that they draw from and a hand that they have cards in and a grave for where all of the destroyed cards go.
